IGREDIENTS CHICKEN RECIPE
  1. cooking oil
  2. chicken masala
  3. royco
  4. enough water
  5. kensalt
  6. carypowder

    Prep the chicken Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Season both sides with salt, pepper, paprika, and herbs. Heat the pan Place a skillet over medium-high heat and add olive oil. Let it get hot (but not smoking). Cook the chicken Add the chicken breasts and cook for about 5–7 minutes without moving them (this helps form a golden crust). Flip and add flavor Flip the chicken, add garlic, and cook another 5–7 minutes until fully cooked (internal temp: 165°F / 75°C). Rest and serve Remove from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes. Optional: squeeze lemon juice on top. Tips Pound thick chicken breasts to even thickness for better cooking. Don’t overcrowd the pan—it lowers the heat and prevents browning. Add a little butter at the end for extra richness.
